## Cross-Site Action List

### Critical (fix before next content push)

**C1 — Meta descriptions missing site-wide**
All 7 pages returned no meta description in extraction. If these are genuinely absent, fix is immediate: Google will auto-generate descriptions from body text, often pulling poor passages. Write a 150–160 char description per page containing the primary keyword and ending with an action phrase.
- Pages affected: all 7
- Meta description suggestions:
  - /pure-1: "Master Cambridge 9709 Pure Mathematics 1 with our complete revision guide. Topic weights, past paper strategy, and 8-week plan. Try ExamPilot free."
  - /integration: "Complete A Level Maths integration guide for Cambridge 9709 Paper 1. Power rule, reverse chain rule, area under curves, and 7 worked examples."
  - /differentiation: "A Level Maths differentiation for Cambridge 9709 Pure 1. Chain rule, stationary points, tangents, normals — with worked examples and exam tips."
  - /functions: "Master A Level Maths functions for Cambridge 9709 Pure 1. Domain, range, composites, inverses, graph transformations — 7 worked examples."
  - /trigonometry: "A Level Maths trigonometry guide for Cambridge 9709 Paper 1. Exact values, CAST diagram, identities, equations — worked examples and exam tips."
  - /quadratics: "A Level Maths quadratics for Cambridge 9709 Pure 1. Completing the square, discriminant, hidden quadratics, inequalities — 6 worked examples."
  - /binomial-series: "A Level Maths series for Cambridge 9709 Pure 1. Binomial expansion, arithmetic and geometric progressions, sum to infinity — 8 worked examples."

**C2 — URL/canonical mismatch on binomial-series page**
Input URL `/cambridge/9709/pure-1/binomial-series` returns a page reporting slug `/cambridge/9709/pure-1/series`. Verify: (a) what the canonical tag points to in source HTML, (b) whether there is a 301 redirect in place. Update the hub page card and all internal links to use the canonical URL consistently.

**C3 — Hub page missing 2 of 6 topic pages**
The /pure-1 hub links to only 4 topic cards (integration, differentiation, functions, trigonometry). Quadratics and binomial-series/series are published but not linked from the hub. This breaks the pillar-spoke architecture and passes no link equity to these pages from the highest-authority page in the cluster.

### Important (fix this sprint)

**I1 — External authority links absent on 5 of 7 pages**
Differentiation, functions, trigonometry, quadratics, and the hub page have zero links to Cambridge Assessment, Ofqual, or Pearson. Integration and binomial-series each have 1 (below the 2–3 target). Add at least 2 per page:
- Cambridge 9709 Mathematics syllabus (cambridgeinternational.org) — universal fit for all pages
- Cambridge examiner reports (2023/2024) for the relevant topic — fits all topic pages that cite examiner errors
- Ofqual grade statistics page — fits any page citing A Level pass rates

**I2 — H2 count exceeds 4–7 on all pages**
All pages have 9–14 H2 headings. Recommended action: collapse some H2s to H3 where they are sub-steps of a larger theme (e.g., "Power Rule," "Reverse Chain Rule," "Definite Integrals" could sit under one H2 "Core Integration Methods"). This also tightens LLM chunk boundaries by reducing orphaned heading clusters.

**I3 — Key Takeaways block absent on all topic pages**
No page has an explicit 3–4 bullet Key Takeaways block immediately after the introduction. This is a GEO requirement (extractable, answer-first structure) and a content quality check. Add to each topic page after the intro:

Example for /integration:
```
Key Takeaways:
• Integration in Cambridge 9709 Paper 1 is worth 15–20 of 75 marks.
• The reverse chain rule only works for linear inner functions (ax + b).
• Always add +C for indefinite integrals — it is marked separately.
• Areas below the x-axis must be calculated by splitting the integral.
```

**I4 — Trigonometry meta title over character limit**
"A Level Maths Trigonometry: Cambridge 9709 Pure 1 Complete Guide" = 63 chars. Google truncates at ~60 chars in SERPs. Shorten to: "A Level Maths Trigonometry: Cambridge 9709 Pure 1 Guide" (55 chars).

**I5 — Missing cross-links between published topic pages**
- /functions does not link to /quadratics (functions are built on quadratic foundations — natural link)
- /quadratics does not link to /integration or /binomial-series
- /binomial-series does not link to /integration, /differentiation, or /functions
- /pure-1 hub does not link to /quadratics or /binomial-series

Each page should link to all directly related published topic pages in the cluster. Current linking is good but selective.

**I6 — Statistics lack named sources with dates**
All 7 pages cite mark allocation percentages ("15–20 marks", "35–40% of Paper 1", etc.) without a named source or data year. Google's E-A-T guidance and the GEO content-standards checklist both require named sources for statistics. Add citations in the format: "(Cambridge International, 9709 Examiner Report [Year])" or link directly to the Cambridge examiner report PDFs.

**I7 — /past-papers URL unverified**
The integration page links to `/past-papers` which is not in the internal-links-map and has no confirmed live status. Verify this page exists or replace the link with a confirmed live URL.
