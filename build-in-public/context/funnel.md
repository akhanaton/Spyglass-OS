# The Builder Funnel

How X / build-in-public fits into ExamPilot's overall growth model. For the student micro-funnel, see `marketing/context/funnel-strategy.md`.

## The Builder Funnel (X / Build in Public)

Separate from the student micro-funnel. Different audience, different goal.

```
X posts/threads (founder credibility)
    "interesting, they're building this"
         |
Personal profile (trust)
    "real people, real progress"
         |
ExamPilot awareness (word of mouth)
    founders tell other founders
         |
Inbound interest (advisors, beta testers, press, partnerships)
```

This funnel does not convert students directly. It builds the credibility layer that makes everything else easier: press coverage, partnership requests, tutor referrals from the edtech community, and a launch-day audience that amplifies the student-facing channels.

| Stage | What happens | Key metric |
|---|---|---|
| Credibility | Consistent posting builds "these people are real" trust | Engagement rate (target 3-6%) |
| Network | Quality replies and threads attract builders at similar stage | Follower growth, DM conversations |
| Amplification | Builder audience retweets launch announcements to their audiences | Impressions on launch content |
| Inbound | Advisors, press, partners reach out because they've followed the journey | Inbound DMs, partnership requests |

## The LinkedIn Funnel (Teresa / Professional Authority)

Separate from the X builder funnel. Different audience, different mechanism, different goal.

```
LinkedIn posts/carousels (educator credibility)
    "this person understands CIE maths teaching"
         |
Teresa's profile (professional trust)
    "educator, parent, building something real"
         |
Teacher/parent discovery (content-led warm-up)
    reading posts before any outreach lands
         |
Outreach → DM → free trial offer
    "I've been following your posts — would 2-3 of your students try this?"
         |
B2C2B referral (one teacher = 50-200 students)
```

This funnel converts differently from X. It is slower (4-6 months to first meaningful referral) but higher-value per connection. One school is worth more than 100 Twitter followers.

| Stage | What happens | Key metric |
|---|---|---|
| Authority | Consistent educator content builds "she knows this space" credibility | Engagement rate (target 3-5%) |
| Discovery | Teachers and parents find Teresa through search and hashtags | Profile views, follower growth |
| Warm-up | Target audience reads 5-10 posts before receiving any DM | Content visible on profile before outreach |
| Outreach | Teresa sends connection requests + DMs to CIE maths teachers | Connection acceptance rate |
| Conversion | Teacher recommends ExamPilot to students/parents | B2C2B referrals attributed to LinkedIn |
