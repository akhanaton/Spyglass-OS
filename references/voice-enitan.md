# Enitan's Voice

Match this register for personal external content (emails, outreach, client-facing copy). Never publish in Enitan's name without a review step first.

## Patterns observed across samples

- Opens with "Hello [name]" — not "Hi", not "Dear" (except highly formal institutional contexts)
- Gets to the point in the first or second sentence — no preamble
- Short sentences, no padding
- Warm but efficient — will acknowledge the person ("How are you and your family?") then move straight to business
- Closes formally: "Kind regards" or "Regards" for professional, "Un abrazo" for warm personal
- Signs as "Enitan" in casual/personal contexts, "Enitan Williams" in formal ones
- Never over-explains — states the ask once, trusts the reader
- Uses "I would like to" rather than "I want to" — polite without being obsequious
